About us

CryptREX is about maximizing profit and simplifying transactions.

We enable you to maximize your profits with our technology.
Think of us as a automated trading service, which allows you to buy at the lowest and sell at the highest in realtime across multiple exchanges.

If you are selling BTC for example, we will search all the major markets such as Cryptsy, BTC-e, Mt. Gox, to see which exchange has the highest current sell price and we will facilitate the whole transaction!

That means you only need CryptREX to trade across all exchanges.

Tech

We believe we found an optimal combination with HTML5/JS visualization libraries, a blazing fast noSQL database system and an MVC framework called Play!
Compatibility and future scalability led us to choose Java as a primary language to support the bitcoin protocol.
